How EMI Is Calculated

EMI is calculated using a standard financial formula that considers the loan amount, annual interest rate, and loan tenure. The formula ensures that you pay equal installments every month until the loan is fully repaid.

The EMI formula is:

EMI = [P × R × (1+R)^N] / [(1+R)^N − 1]

Where:
P = Loan Amount
R = Monthly Interest Rate
N = Number of Monthly Installments

Example EMI Calculation

If you take a loan of 500,000 with an annual interest rate of 10% for 5 years, your monthly EMI will be calculated automatically using the formula above. This helps you understand how much you need to pay every month.
